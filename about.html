About Me

Hi, I'm Sandaru! I'm an undergraduate student at SIBA Campus pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSc IT). I am passionate about technology, programming, and creating innovative solutions to real-world problems. I am always looking for opportunities to learn and grow as a developer and a professional.

I am also actively volunteering with the Zero Plastic Club, working alongside the SIBA Zero Plastic Community. As the president of this community, I am committed to leading efforts towards environmental sustainability. I enjoy singing, and I can play the violin, which adds to my creative skills. I also have a deep interest in video editing and graphic design, constantly looking for new ways to express myself and develop my skills. I am always eager to explore new things and take on challenges that will help me grow both personally and professionally.
